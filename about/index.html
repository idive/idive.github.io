About
The iDive was developed in the Red Sea Research Center at KAUST to provide scientists with better ways to record data while scuba diving. But once we had figured out how to use an iPad underwater it seemed a shame not to let everyone else in on the fun.

There are many ways to waterproof a product, but there's no product that allows you to use the full capabilities of the touchscreen while underwater. Whether you're recording data, taking photos, or playing Angry Birds during your safety stop, iDive helps you do what you need to do, underwater.
