Features
iDive's core enabling technology is the Balance Module that internally pressurizes the casing and retains the spacing between casing membrane and electronic device screen as you go dive deeper.
See it work, depth by depth.
1
Above Water
The iDive is fully functional without any internal pressure adjustments.
2
At 1m deep
The iDive, and other similar waterproof casings will work just under the surface without any pressure adjustments.
3
At 2m and deeper
The iDive sets itself apart from the competition when pressure at depths comes into the equation. We have tested the iDive down to as deep as 100 ft to date, able to use the touch screen fully.
